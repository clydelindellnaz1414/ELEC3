About Us

Welcome to Chicken Pastil House, the home of authentic, flavor-packed pastil in P5 Calzada, Guinobatan. Our recipe is rooted in tradition, using only the freshest chicken and spices sourced from local markets to bring the comforting taste of home to every bite.

We believe in quality over quantity, preparing fresh batches daily to ensure every pastil meets our high standards. From our family to yours, we're proud to serve Albay with love and dedication.

Mission & Vision

🎯 Mission

To delight our community by serving high-quality, budget-friendly pastil that never compromises on taste. We aim to preserve Bicolano culinary traditions while making them accessible to everyone in Guinobatan and beyond.

👁️ Vision

To be the leading choice for Filipino comfort food in Albay, known for our signature flavors, unwavering quality, and deep respect for local heritage. We envision expanding our reach while maintaining the heart and soul of our homemade recipes.

FAQ

🥘 Are ingredients fresh?
Absolutely! We prepare fresh batches daily using locally sourced chicken and ingredients. Nothing frozen, nothing processed - just authentic, home-cooked quality.

📦 Do you take bulk orders?
Yes! We cater to events, parties, and gatherings. Please contact us at least 1 day in advance for orders of 20+ pieces to ensure availability.

🕐 What are your operating hours?
We're open daily from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Early birds get the freshest batches!

🚗 Do you deliver?
Currently, we serve walk-in customers at P1 Bololo. For delivery, please coordinate through our Messenger or phone.

🌶️ How spicy is the Spicy Pastil?
Our standard is moderately spicy (perfect kick!), but you can customize the heat level from mild to "pinakasilyo" - just let us know!
